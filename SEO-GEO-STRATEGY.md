# After Bites SEO and GEO Strategy

Goal: rank on the first page of Google India for "paan mukhwas" and related
searches, and get quoted by AI answer engines (Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT,
Gemini, Perplexity) when people ask about paan mukhwas or mouth freshener.

Store: https://www.theafterbites.com
Brand: After Bites (Surat, Gujarat, India)
Products: Wet Paan Mukhwas, Dry Paan Mukhwas (500g and 800g)

Note on style: all customer facing copy here avoids long dashes and short
dashes. Separators use a pipe or a comma only.

---

## 1. What SEO and GEO mean here

SEO (Search Engine Optimization): getting blue link rankings on Google and
Bing for people who type "paan mukhwas" and similar terms.

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization): getting the brand named and quoted
inside AI generated answers. AI engines pull from pages that have clear
entities, structured data, direct question and answer text, and facts they
can lift as a sentence. Much of GEO overlaps with good SEO, plus a few extra
moves listed in section 6.

## 5. On page actions (SEO)

1. Front load keywords in product titles. Format:
   Product name, benefit, then brand. Example without dashes:
   "Wet Paan Mukhwas, Meetha Paan Mouth Freshener | After Bites".
2. Fill product tags from section 3.
3. Build a Paan Mukhwas collection page with 150 to 300 words of intro copy
   using the head keyword and support terms.
4. Keep meta descriptions near 155 characters with the keyword early.
5. Add descriptive alt text to product images, for example
   "Wet paan mukhwas tobacco free mouth freshener jar 500g".
6. Add 2 to 4 blog guides for the question keywords in section 3e.
7. Internal links: home to collection to product, blog to product.
8. Keep one clear H1 per page that contains the primary keyword.

---

## 6. GEO actions (AI answer visibility)

1. Answer questions in plain sentences. The FAQ block already helps.
   Add a short "What is paan mukhwas" answer in the first 100 words of the
   collection page so an AI can quote it.
2. State facts an AI can lift: "100 percent tobacco free and supari free",
   "made in Surat, Gujarat", "available in 500g and 800g".
3. Keep name, address, phone identical everywhere (site, Google Business,
   social profiles). Consistency builds the entity.
4. Use structured data already in place. Keep it valid.
5. Create a comparison and a benefits article. AI engines favor pages that
   compare options and list clear benefits.
6. Encourage reviews. Rating count and review text feed both SEO and GEO.
7. Register and complete a Google Business Profile for local and entity trust.

---

## 7. Off page and technical

1. Google Search Console: verify the domain, submit the sitemap
   (/sitemap.xml), watch the Links report for backlinks.
2. Bing Webmaster Tools: same setup for Bing and Copilot.
3. Google Business Profile: create it with the Surat address.
4. Backlinks: get listed on Indian food and D2C directories, do brand
   collaborations, and reach out to mukhwas and snack roundup blogs.
5. Page speed: keep images sized and lazy loaded.
6. Mobile first: most India traffic is mobile. Keep the product page fast.

---

## 8. Priority order

1. Fill product tags and refine product titles and meta. (done in this pass)
2. Verify Google Search Console and submit sitemap.
3. Build the Paan Mukhwas collection landing page with intro copy.
4. Create a Google Business Profile.
5. Publish 2 to 4 blog guides for the question keywords.
6. Start backlink outreach.

Ranking for a head term like "paan mukhwas" takes months of content, links,
and reviews. The long tail terms in 3d can rank first, often within weeks,
and bring buyers while the head term builds.
