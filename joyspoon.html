Discover the perfect combination of indulgence and health with Joyspoon's Til Mulkhwas – a traditional mouth freshener crafted from sweetened sesame seeds. Packed with natural flavors and the goodness of sesame, it’s your go-to treat for a refreshing post-meal experience.
